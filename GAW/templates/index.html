Urban Garbage Detection System

Solid Waste Accumulation Challenge

Solid waste accumulation is one of the most pressing environmental challenges in urban areas. Our advanced AI-powered detection system uses gas sensor data to identify and analyze garbage presence in real-time. By detecting methane, CO₂, ammonia, and hydrogen sulfide levels, we can determine waste contamination patterns and assess environmental risk levels. This innovative approach helps municipalities and waste management facilities optimize collection routes and respond to health hazards more effectively.
